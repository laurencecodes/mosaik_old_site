What we do

We’re empowering a community of refugees to shape and lead programmes that improve access to university

We started co-design workshops in 2018 so young refugees can have a say on what their programmes look like.

This year We‘ll work across 2 countries, 5 towns and cities, reaching over 200 young refugees, 6 local community centres, and thousands more online.


Meet our team

Our programmes

Guidance workshops

Our Workshops help refugees find the university pathways best suited to them, set goals, and work on achieving them.

Peer-to-peer learning

Peer-to-peer learning programmes are a platform to share advice, support, and stories about accessing university.

English classes

We emphasise dialogue and draw on student experiences, making content relevant to refugees’ lives. Our classes don’t rely on expensive course materials.
