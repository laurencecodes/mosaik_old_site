Helping refugees reach university

If you’re a refugee looking for support join our Facebook group or email hello@mosaik.ngo

Only 1% of refugees reach university the global average is 37%

University lets refugees contribute to local economies, increase their self-sufficiency and support their families

How we help

Guidance and courses designed with refugees to work for them

Programmes made scalable and accessible using technology

Working with organisations in Jordan and Lebanon

Case studies

Batoul from Syria

Batoul, with a guest dietician, hosted Facebook Live sessions on how to stay focused during Ramadan.

In 2019, student led programmes like this have reached 813 young people in Jordan and Lebanon.

Hamza from Sudan

“In the class I’m not just learning English, I’m developing my critical thinking.”

Our methods of language learning emphasise dialogue and draws on student experiences making class content relevant to refugees’ lives.
