Moodle administrator

About the role

  • Manage courses and user accounts across multiple Moodle installations and versions.
  • To be responsible for the on-going Moodle virtual learning environment/ learning management system.
  • Update and host new learning materials for a three month course starting in January 2020.
  • Support colleagues, external partners and learners in the effective use of Moodle and addressing any issues they are facing.
  • Address installation, configuration, registration and enrollment issues.
  • Train new staff members and update the training materials if needed (Remote or face to face training).
  • Provide data and reporting when needed.
  • Manage (and update when needed) course documents stored on a shared network drive and maintain effective version control.

About you

  • Experienced in a similar role or a qualification in a related discipline.
  • Experience administration a virtual learning environment such as Moodle/Blackboard etc.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills will come naturally to you.
  • You will enjoy working in a team but also be able to show that when required you can work independently.
  • You will be confident in managing a variety of demands and changing priorities and be able to meet deadlines.
  • Fluency in Arabic & English.

Desirable skills and experience

  • Experience of designing resources, activities and assessment for face-to-face, online distance and blended learners.
  • Designing skills (photoshop or any similar tool).
  • Experience with Articulate 360.
  • Experience with other LMS than Moodle.

Location

The successful candidate will be able to work remotely.

Contract

Part-time, equivalent to 0.25 F/T for 3.5 months with possibility for extension; anticipated start mid-December 2019; £250 - £400 per month, depending on experience.

Get in touch

Please send a CV and cover letter, citing examples of previous work using Moodle/Blackboard/other LMS, to hello@mosaik.ngo by 22 November 2019. Candidates are encouraged to apply as early as possible as applications will be screened prior to the vacancy deadline.
